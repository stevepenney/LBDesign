NZ-Style Beam Elevation - Interactive Demo

Click to test the interactive SVG beam designer

Interactive Features:

  • Span: Click "span" dimension below beam to edit
  • Loads: Click the brown "X.XX kN/m" text to edit LL, DL, SDL, and spacing
  • Point Load Position: Drag the wall section OR click the dimension (PL1, PL2, etc.)
  • Point Load Magnitude: Click the "X.X kN" label above the wall
  • Add Point Load: Use "+ Add Point Load" button in toolbar
  • Spacing: For joists/rafters, click spacing in toolbar OR in load popup
